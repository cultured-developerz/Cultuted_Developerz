Data regarding the household wastes and their alternatives

  • Wastes

    • Organic

  • Harmful Effects

    • Organic wastes produced at home generally food wastes if not disposed properly produce harmful greenhouse gases which affect our health and nature.
  • Alternatives

    • Reduce food wastes as much as possible. Organic wastes can also be used for providing nutrients to plants for their growth.
    Plastics

    Plastics are very harmful to the environment as they are non-biodegradable.
    Plastic waste produces greenhouse gas emissions during every stage of its
    lifecycle. The extraction and transportation of plastic is dependent on oil, gas, and coal. The production and improper disposal of plastics also release tons of carbon emissions.
    Reduce the use of plastic as much as possible and reuse the ones already at hand. And use other alternatives such as : paper bags, cloth bags etc.
